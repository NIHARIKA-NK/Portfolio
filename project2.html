Project Overview

The Canteen App streamlines the process of ordering food, making payments, and managing dining services at canteens.

My Role

Designer responsible for ensuring smooth navigation and modern UI elements to enhance user experience.

Challenges

Creating a design that caters to both first-time and experienced users while minimizing wait times in busy environments.

Outcome

The app reduced operational overheads and improved user satisfaction, making it a must-have for modern dining.
